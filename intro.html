What are Mindscapes?

Mindscape is the first mind-mapping tool to visualise intuitive thinking

Intuition is one's ability to acquire knowledge without rational reasoning or external interference. It is a faculty generated by the unconscious mind encompassing our conscious ability to know valid solutions to problems and decision making.

When you are standing on top of a mountain, the landscape is expansively spread out in front of you: hills, valleys, rivers, and lakes... In a similar way, a "mindscape" provides a map so that we can "see" our thinking. It is as if we were standing outside our own thinking and looking at it objectively.

Why are Mindscapes important?

Mindscapes are the snapshots of our inner world

The process of creating a mindcape, or mindscaping, is called self-inquery, a mindfulness practice that directs attention to the inner awareness of I. Mindscape as a visual tool, provides an alternative to what it would be like to create a cartographic journal of thoughts, allowing individuals to explore the thought patterns and relationships from one another. This practice can be helpful for anyone interested in learning more about themselves.
